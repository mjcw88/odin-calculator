# The Odin Project (Foundations) - Calculator

Calculator project for the Odin Project, featuring use of objects, keyboard functionality and extensive logicstical conditionals to account for edge cases. For sake of convienience, the calculator maxes out at 15 digits (positive and negative) and maxes out at 3 decimal places.

#### Features

##### C
    Clears the current display (C on the keyboard)
#### AC
    Clears everything from the calculators memory and starts fresh (all clear) (A on the keyboard)
#### +/−
    Inverts the number from positive to negative and vice versa. (I on the keyboard)
#### ⌫
    Backspace, deletes the last character in the display. (Backspace on the keyboard)
#### ÷
    Division (/ on the keyboard)
#### ×
    Multiplication (* on the keyboard)
#### −
    Subtraction (- on the keyboard)
#### +
    Addition (+ on the keyboard)
#### =
    Equals (Enter on the keyboard)
